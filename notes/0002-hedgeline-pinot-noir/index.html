Hedgeline Vinyards Pinot Noir

  • Vintage 2013
  • Price $12
  • Origin

Thoughts

This wine is actually made in California by Winepress Vintners (probably part of a big conglomerate), but it is made from Oregon Pinot Noir grapes. This was a really enjoyable and affordable wine and I would buy again for sure.

Tasting Notes

  • Light ruby in color with tints of brown around the edges
  • Lots of fresh red fruit and floral aromas
  • Light body
  • Caramel and almond notes in the finish
